FinancialsExpression
A new composite FinancialsExpression that consists of this FinancialsExpression added to the indicated FinancialsExpression. This composite is a new expression that is the mathematical sum or difference of the values of the two expressions.
You can wrap a FinancialsExpression in a FinancialsCalculation or a FinancialsCalculator in order to evaluate its amount.

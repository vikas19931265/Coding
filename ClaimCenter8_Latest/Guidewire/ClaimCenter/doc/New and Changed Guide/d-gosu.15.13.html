Interceptors Removed

Before ClaimCenter 7.0, there was a feature called interceptors that allowed you to trigger custom code before or after method invocation. The mechanism was through annotations that implement the IPreMethodInterceptor, and IPostMethodInterceptor interfaces. This mechanism was removed as part of the change to bytecode-compiled Gosu in ClaimCenter 7.0.

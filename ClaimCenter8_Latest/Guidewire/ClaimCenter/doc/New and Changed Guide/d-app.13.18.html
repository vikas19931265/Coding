Split Payment During Recode

Previous to ClaimCenter 7.0, it was possible to recode only a payment with a different reserve line. In ClaimCenter 7.0, it is now also possible to split a payment and its amount into multiple payments on different reserve lines.
To access the payment recode functionality, do the following:
1.
Select a claim.
2.
Click Financials in the left-hand links.
3.
Click a payment amount to open the Transactions screen.
4.
Click the Amount link of a Payment transaction. This action opens the Payment Details screen.
5.
Click Recode.
In the Recode Payment screen, you can do the following:
•
Change the reserve line (pre-existing functionality).
•
Enter a comment (pre-existing functionality).
•
Add additional line items and set the line category and amount for each line item (pre-existing functionality).
•
Click Add Payment and then edit the reserve line and amounts of the payments to reflect the new reserve line and amount on each. Their amounts must add up to the original payment's amount (new functionality).

Changing Your Password in ContactManager

To change your password
1.
Log in as described at “Logging in to ContactManager”.
2.
On the Options menu , click Preferences.
The Preferences worksheet opens below the main work area.
3.
Enter your old password.
4.
Enter the new password.
5.
Enter the new password again in the Confirm New Password field.
6.
Click Update.

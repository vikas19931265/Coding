RIAgreement (cc_riagreement) (delegates to Extractable, Retireable, Validatable)
Description
Represents the contractual obligation of a reinsurer who has taken on some of the policy risk in exchange for some of the premium. The RIAgreement centralizes information related to that contract, detailing how the risk has been divided and ceded. Additionally, there is information to indicate when alerts should be directed to counterparties and users.

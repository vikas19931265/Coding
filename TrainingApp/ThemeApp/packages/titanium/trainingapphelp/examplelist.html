TrainingApp v7.0.2-5 (May, 2012)

Welcome to TrainingApp

TrainingApp is an application built specifically to train people on the fundamentals of Guidewire configuration and integration. As a starting point, it has several advantages over the genuine Guidewire applications:

  • Its business functionality does not require detailed knowledge of insurance.

  • It has at least one simple example of all the fundamental Guidewire configuration and integration functionality.

  • It is a smaller application, which means it can be (re)started more rapidly than the other applications.

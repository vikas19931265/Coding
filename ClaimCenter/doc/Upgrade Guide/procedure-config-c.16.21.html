Linking Lienholders to Location-based Risk Units

ClaimCenter 7.0 and newer allow a lienholder to be linked to a location-based risk unit. A lienholder must be linked to a location but can also be linked to a location-based risk unit to make it specific to the risk unit. Guidewire improved the user interface to show lienholders that are related to the location-based risk unit, rather than just lienholders related to the location.

ClaimCenter Application Guide : Additional Features of ClaimCenter : Working with Activities

Working with Activities

ClaimCenter tracks all tasks, or units of work, involved in handling a claim. Actions such as inspecting a vehicle, reviewing medical information, negotiating with the claimant, making payments, and so on are called activities.
Activities are the central mechanism for tracking completion of all varieties of tasks. ClaimCenter divides the work for a claim into activities and provides a list of these activities to enable you to track them to completion. These activities track everything that must be done to settle every claim.
The claim segmentation process creates an initial set of activities for a new claim. Additional activities can be added to the claim at any time. Multiple users can be assigned activities on a single claim. Assigned activities represent units of work for the claim and enable the work units to be divided among users.
Tracking work by using activities enables claim owners to perform all necessary claim-handling tasks and identify missed tasks. Supervisors and managers can also track assigned work and identify problem claims, such as claims with many overdue or escalated activities.

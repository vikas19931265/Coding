Differences Between Notes and Documents

Notes and documents have distinct functions in ClaimCenter, and the application handles them differently. The following table highlights the main differences. See also “Document Management”.
 
Characteristics of Notes
Characteristics of Documents
Written in plain text.
Can have many different MIME types, such as PDF, Word, or Excel.
Created by a user or through Gosu.
Created by a user, or through Gosu, or in an external document management system.
Stored only in the ClaimCenter database.
Stored either in the ClaimCenter database or in a document management system.
Related to one claim or claim entity.
Can be related to one claim, linked to many others, and attached to notes.

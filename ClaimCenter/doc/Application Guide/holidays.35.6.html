ClaimCenter Application Guide : Additional Features of ClaimCenter : Holidays and Business Weeks : Holiday Permissions

Holiday Permissions

The following system permissions control whether you can view the Holidays screen and edit the holidays:
•
holidayview
•
holidaymanage
In the base configuration, the Superuser role has these permissions.

Business Week Permissions

The following system permissions control whether you can view and edit the Business Week screen:
•
buswkview
•
buswkmanage
In the base configuration, the Superuser role has these permissions.

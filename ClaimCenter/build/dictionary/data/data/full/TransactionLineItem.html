TransactionLineItem (cc_transactionlineitem) (delegates to Extractable, Retireable)
Description
TransactionLineItems provide a means to split the amount of a transaction into multiple categories (e.g., Doctor, Hospital, Legal, Other, etc.). Every transaction has one or more TransactionLineItems, and the amount of the transaction is the aggregate amount of all its line items.
